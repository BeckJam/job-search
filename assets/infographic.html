Product Brief
v1.0 // March 2026

Job Search Agent Team

AI-orchestrated application toolkit that generates tailored resumes and cover letters from a single career reference, with human review at every stage.
Problem

Customizing a resume and cover letter for each role takes 6+ hours. Paid tools ($100+/mo) produce generic output that still needs full rewrites. Most of the time is spent on mechanical work, not storytelling.

Solution

10 specialized AI agents orchestrated via Claude Code. Parallel keyword analysis, company research, and document generation, with human-in-the-loop approval at every stage. Your career, your voice, your final call.
