## STAR Projects

<!-- Each major achievement as a detailed STAR story. These are the building blocks the writing agents use to craft compelling bullets and cover letter paragraphs. Include 5-15 projects. -->

### Project Name

- **Situation**: What was the context? What problem existed?
- **Task**: What were you specifically responsible for?
- **Action**: What did you do? Be specific about your decisions, technologies, and approach.
- **Result**: What was the measurable outcome? Include metrics: revenue, cost savings, time reduction, user growth, etc.
- **Key Metrics**: List the headline numbers (e.g., "$2M cost reduction", "40% faster delivery", "15,000 users onboarded")
- **Keywords**: Relevant terms for ATS matching (e.g., "cloud migration", "agile transformation", "stakeholder management")
